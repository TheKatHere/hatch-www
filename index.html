What is Hatch?

Hatch is a third-party project hosting service for projects made using Scratch and TurboWarp. Scratch is a great option to learn the fundamentals of programming, but it is designed for young children. Many Scratchers understandably find themselves outgrowing Scratch as they get older, and/or have distaste for new Scratch features, but they want to keep using the Scratch editor to create projects.

Hatch was designed with this group of users in mind. While we're very similar to Scratch, having a close online community, we have more relaxed rules, allowing for more content types and genres that may not be suitable for the Scratch website.

Why join Hatch?

Community Features

Hatch is community focused, with social features for user pages and project pages.

Dedicated Developers

Our developers work to make Hatch the best it can be based off feedback from Hatch members. Check out our forums where you can leave a suggestion!

Support for Advanced Projects

Hatch plays projects using TurboWarp which allows for more functionality than traditional Scratch projects through custom extensions and settings.

Less Restrictions

Since Hatch is aimed at teenagers 13 and older, we have less restrictions on content in place than Scratch, allowing for many genres of projects to be shared.*

*Due to some content on Hatch being unsuitable for younger audiences, please DO NOT speak of or link to Hatch anywhere on the Scratch website, as it would go against the Scratch Community Guidelines. Thank you for your understanding of and cooperation with this important matter.
