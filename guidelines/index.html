Guidelines
Please see our Terms of Use for more elaboration on each rule.
1. No 18+ or NSFW Content
2. No slurs or extreme profanity.
3. No discriminatory content against certain people or groups.
4. No stolen or copyrighted content.
5. No 'doxxing' or exposing the personally identifiable information of any user (including your own, to an extent). This includes but is not limited to home addresses, IP addresses, and phone numbers.
6. No providing content on illegal activities.
7. No posting content only to anger other members or get attention.
8. Do not impersonate anyone who you are not.
9. Generally, be a nice person. Targeted bullying or harassment isn't okay.
